Privacy Policy

Last updated: April, 2025

At Hisho & Kanri Pvt Ltd, your privacy is important to us. We collect, use, and protect your personal data with the highest standards to ensure transparency and trust.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and company details
  • Usage behavior on our platform including pages visited and interaction history
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, and operating system
  • Uploaded files or documentation shared with us for service delivery
How We Use It
  • To provide and manage our accounting, financial, and compliance services
  • To personalize the user experience and improve our platform
  • To send relevant updates, newsletters, and service-related communications
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations
Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to track your activity and enhance user experience. Cookies help us understand usage patterns, store preferences, and improve our service offerings. You can choose to disable cookies via your browser settings.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information with trusted partners, affiliates, or regulatory bodies when necessary to fulfill services or comply with legal obligations. All third parties are contractually bound to protect your data.

Data Security

We implement strong technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data, including encryption, firewalls, and secure data storage. While we strive for maximum security, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights
  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • You may request a copy of your data or ask us to stop using it
  • For any request, please contact our Data Protection Officer at admin@hiskan.com
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. We encourage you to review it regularly.

Referral Policy

Last updated: April, 2025

We appreciate you spreading the word! Our referral program is designed to reward those who help grow the Hisho & Kanri Pvt Ltd community. Please review the following terms carefully:

Eligibility

Only existing clients or registered users are eligible to refer others. The referred party must be a new client who has not previously engaged with our services under any identity.

Reward

Upon successful onboarding and payment by the referred client, the referrer will receive a reward in the form of a discount, service credit, or e-voucher — as specified in the current referral campaign.

Reward Conditions
  • Rewards will be processed within 30 days of successful sign-up and payment.
  • Only one referrer is eligible for each new client referral.
  • Rewards are non-transferable and not redeemable for cash unless otherwise stated.
Program Changes

We reserve the right to modify, pause, or terminate the referral program at any time without prior notice. Changes will be posted on this page.

Abuse

Any form of abuse, such as creating fake accounts, self-referrals, or misrepresentation, will lead to disqualification from the referral program and may result in suspension of services.

Transparency

Referrers must clearly disclose that they may receive a reward when promoting Hisho & Kanri Pvt Ltd to others. Transparency builds trust and keeps our community strong.

By participating in the referral program, you agree to these terms and confirm that your referrals will be made in good faith.

Referral Limits

There is no limit to how many people you can refer. However, rewards may be capped monthly or annually based on promotional guidelines. Any limits will be clearly communicated in the referral campaign details.

Tax Implications

Depending on your region (Singapore, Malaysia, India), referral rewards may be subject to income tax or reporting obligations. It is your responsibility to comply with applicable tax laws and report rewards as required.

Verification & Approval

All referrals undergo verification to ensure they meet the eligibility criteria. Hisho & Kanri Pvt Ltd reserves the right to approve or deny referrals based on internal checks and discretion.

Regional Availability

This referral program is currently valid for clients and referrals within Singapore, Malaysia, and India. Expansion to other regions will be notified via email or on our website.

Legal Compliance

By participating in the referral program, you agree to abide by applicable referral laws and anti-spam regulations in your region. Misuse may be prosecuted under relevant laws.

Fair Use

This program is designed to reward genuine, word-of-mouth referrals. Any attempt to exploit or manipulate the program, including incentivizing referrals without disclosing terms, may result in disqualification.
